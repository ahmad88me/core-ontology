Ensemblec back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://postdata.linhd.uned.es/ontology/postdata-core#Ensemble

IRI: http://postdata.linhd.uned.es/ontology/postdata-core#PoeticWork

Abstract concept of an artistic creation. In POSTDATA these creations must be in verse (poem, play, song) Eg.: Os Lusíadas by Luis Vaz de Camões; La Commedia by Dante; the poem Ad te deus by an Anonymous author written in between the 7th and 9th centuries
